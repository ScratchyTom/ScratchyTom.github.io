About Donald

Professional bio, minus the fluff

Short bio

Donald Lemke is a children’s book editor and writer who’s penned 100+ titles for young readers—from board books to middle grade—often in the worlds of iconic, kid-beloved brands.

Long bio

Donald Lemke is a children’s book editor and writer with 100+ published titles spanning board books, picture books, and middle grade. His work blends kid-forward humor with strong concepts and tight execution—whether he’s inventing interactive formats, building a playful mission around everyday kid milestones, or translating big-brand worlds into collectible products readers want to keep. He lives in St. Paul, Minnesota.
